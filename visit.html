Plan Your Visit

We're excited to welcome you to our church family. Whether this is your first time or you're returning, we want to make your visit meaningful and comfortable.

What to Expect

Your first visit should be comfortable and welcoming. Here's what you need to know.

Dress Code

Come as you are! Whether you prefer casual or formal attire, you'll feel welcome. We believe what matters most is your heart, not your outfit.

Parking

Free parking is available in our main lot off Faith Street. Additional street parking is available on surrounding streets. Accessible parking spots are near the main entrance.

Children & Youth

We offer age-appropriate programs during all services: nursery (0-2), children's church (3-12), and youth group (13-18). All volunteers are background-checked.

Service Length

Our services typically last 60-75 minutes, including contemporary worship, prayer, and a practical biblical message. Feel free to arrive a few minutes early to find a seat.

Coffee & Welcome

Join us 30 minutes before service for coffee and refreshments in our welcome center. Our greeting team is here to answer questions and help you feel at home.

Accessibility

Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps and accessible restrooms. Hearing assistance devices are available at the welcome desk.
