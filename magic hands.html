About Us

Hope Family Spirit Filled Assembly Int'l is a religious organization with the mandate of adding values and bringing hope to lives and destinies through the preaching of the Word.

Date of Commission

Birthed on 2nd February 2020 in Ekpoma, Edo State, Nigeria

Senior Pastors

Rev. Dr. Godwin & Pastor Mrs. Veronica Akele

Seasoned ministers with a heart for God's people and a passion for national & international transformation.

Our Vision Statement

  • To raise a people of hope in this hopeless generation.
  • To bring hope to homes, families, businesses, and careers.
  • To make Jesus Christ known — our Hope of Glory.
  • To establish men and women on the power of hope.
  • To raise people with the hope of eternity.

Our Mission Statement

  • To make disciples of all nations.
  • To touch lives through the preaching of the Gospel of Hope.
  • To add value to destinies.
  • To take the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the nations through media, tracts, and literature materials.
  • To raise a godly children's ministry.
  • To build godly families among our members.
  • To establish educational academies.
  • To show hospitality to orphans, widows, and the less privileged.
