REV DR GODWIN AKELE

LEAD PASTOR

Biography

Rev Dr Godwin Akele is a distinguished servant of God, ordained minister, and the General Overseer of Hope Family Spirit filled Assembly. With over two decades of dedicated ministry experience, he has touched countless lives through his powerful preaching, compassionate leadership, and unwavering commitment to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Born with a passion for ministry, Rev Dr Akele answered the call to serve God at an early age. He pursued theological education with excellence, earning his Doctorate in Theology and Ministry Leadership. His academic achievements are complemented by practical ministry experience that spans local church leadership, international missions, and community development initiatives.

Under his leadership, HOPE FAMILY has experienced tremendous growth both spiritually and numerically. His vision for a church that not only worships together but also serves the community has led to the establishment of numerous outreach programs, including feeding initiatives, educational support, and counseling services.

Rev Dr Akele is known for his dynamic preaching style that combines biblical depth with practical application. His messages resonate with people from all walks of life, bringing hope to the hopeless, healing to the broken, and direction to those seeking purpose. He believes that the church should be a beacon of light in the community, demonstrating God's love through both words and actions.

Beyond the pulpit, he is a loving husband of Pst Mrs,Veronica Akele and devoted father of 4 kids, 2 boys and 2 girls who exemplifies the values he preaches. His family serves alongside him in ministry, demonstrating the importance of family unity and godly leadership in the home. Rev Dr Akele is also an accomplished author, having written books on Relationship issues and spiritual growth .
